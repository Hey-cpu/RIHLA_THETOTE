About Rihla

Rihla is a sustainable tote bag made entirely from recycled textile waste and crafted by local women tailors. The name "Rihla" means "journey" — symbolizing a journey towards a greener, more conscious lifestyle.

Features

  • Made from 100% recycled textile waste
  • Removable laptop pouch for versatility
  • Dual bottle sleeves for easy organization
  • Wide, comfortable straps for everyday use
  • Unique QR code on every bag linking to your personal eco-impact
  • Hand-stitched by empowered local women

Customization

Personalize your Rihla tote with fabric choices, patches, and messages. Design online or join our school workshops to handcraft your own unique bag. Express yourself sustainably!

Our Environmental Impact

Every Rihla bag helps reduce textile waste by up to 0.6 kg. By choosing Rihla, you support the circular economy, empower women tailors, and promote sustainability education.
